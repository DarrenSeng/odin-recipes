Description

Light, fresh, and flavorful, this Chicken Caesar Salad is a perfect choice for a quick and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Grilled Chicken Breast
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Cherry Tomatoes (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients needed for Chicken Caesar Salad, including Romaine lettuce, grilled chicken breast, croutons, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing, and cherry tomatoes (optional).
  2. Wash and chop the Romaine lettuce. Slice the grilled chicken breast into strips.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, grilled chicken strips, croutons, and cherry tomatoes. Toss the salad ingredients together.
  4. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss again until well coated.
  5. Serve your refreshing Chicken Caesar Salad as a light and satisfying meal option!
