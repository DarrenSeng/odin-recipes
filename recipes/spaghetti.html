Description

Delicious and hearty, this Spaghetti Bolognese recipe is a classic comfort food that everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Herbs and Spices
  • Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients needed for Spaghetti Bolognese, including spaghetti, ground beef, tomato sauce, onions, garlic, herbs, and spices. Optionally, prepare Parmesan cheese for topping.
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. In a pan, saut√© onions and garlic, then add ground beef and cook until browned. Pour in tomato sauce, season with herbs and spices, and let it simmer.
  3. Once the sauce is ready, serve it over the cooked spaghetti. Top with Parmesan cheese if desired.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Bolognese! This savory dish is perfect for a satisfying meal.
